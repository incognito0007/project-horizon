# 📓 horizon-notes

> Daily logs · weekly reflections · resources · things that clicked and things that didn't.

Part of [Project Horizon](../README.md) — a 5-month structured engineering growth journey.

---

## What lives here

The human side of the journey. Every week gets a Markdown file. No polish required — these are honest, in-the-moment notes written at the end of each session.

```
horizon-notes/
├── weekly-logs/
│   ├── week-01.md
│   ├── week-02.md
│   └── ...
├── resources.md        # Curated links — docs, videos, articles worth revisiting
├── concepts/           # Deeper write-ups on things worth documenting properly
│   ├── react-rendering.md
│   ├── csharp-async.md
│   └── ...
└── retrospectives/
    ├── month-1.md
    ├── month-2.md
    └── ...
```

---

## Weekly log format

Each `week-XX.md` follows a loose template — fill in what's useful, skip what isn't:

```markdown
# Week XX — [dates]

## What I worked on

- Monday: ...
- Tuesday: ...
- Wednesday: ...
- Thursday: ...
- Friday: ...

## What clicked

Something that finally made sense this week.

## What I'm still fuzzy on

Honest list — no hiding.

## Best resource this week

A link, a video, a section of a book.

## One thing I'd tell next-week-me
```

## Monthly retrospectives

At the end of each month, a slightly longer reflection in `retrospectives/`:

- What improved most this month?
- What fell behind — and why?
- What does next month need to go differently?
- Anything to add or remove from the plan?
